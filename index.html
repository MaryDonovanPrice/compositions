Mary Price

I would rather write 10,000 notes than a single letter of the alphabet.

-Beethoven























A well trained musician and composer that studied French horn at Georgia College & State University and composition at the Peabody Institute. Performed in several types of ensembles such as French horn in concert band, orchestra, and brass ensemble; trumpet in jazz band; percussion ensemble; tenor in chorus; and laptop ensembles. Composed and arranged many types of performance including small ensemble, voice, full orchestra, and solo. Also, contemporary ensembles with atypical combinations of instruments and acoustic-electric ensembles. Recorded, edited, and directed students.
